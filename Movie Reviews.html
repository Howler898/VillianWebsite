The Movies

By Aidan Farmilo

Baby Driver

Baby Driver is an amazing film filled with action and comedy. It makes the audience think and it's very easy to relate to the characters. It also has some big names in it.

Thor Ragnarok

Thor Ragnarok is hilarious and easily my favorite of the three Thor films. Taika Waititi uses the material perfectly to bring comedy and action together making an amazing movie.

John Wick

John Wick manages to catch the audiences attention with the amazing effects and brutal violence but also has loss in it and makes the audience empathise with a serial killer.

21 Jump Street

21 Jump Street is a hilarious comedy about 2 new cops who have to go undercover. It managed to make me laugh and by the end I really liked the main characters and their story.

22 Jump Street

This is the second entry in the Jump Street franchise. It is a pretty good movie though not as good as the first. It tries to re-do some of the same jokes as the last one but falls short.

Kingsmen: The Secret Service

Kingsmen is personally one of my favorite movies as it brings in the thrilling action of being a spy and makes the audience relate with the main character while keeping him exciting.
